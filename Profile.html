PROFILE


Son Seung Wan

Member of Korean Girl Group Red Velvet

Son Seung-wan (born February 21, 1994), known professionally as Wendy, is a South Korean singer. She was born in Seongbuk-dong, Seoul, South Korea. She is a member and main vocalist of the South Korean girl group Red Velvet.
